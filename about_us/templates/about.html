About Us

Burger King, a global fast-food chain, is renowned for its flame-grilled burgers. Founded in 1954, it's known for its signature Whopper sandwich, often customizable with various toppings. The menu extends beyond burgers to include chicken sandwiches, fries, salads, and desserts. With a focus on convenience and speed, Burger King strives to offer a diverse range of tasty, affordable options for customers seeking quick-service meals.
